## â“ FAQ

### Q: Do I need to select filters?

**A:** No! All filters are optional. You can generate the report without selecting anything.

### Q: What if I don't see the Print button?

**A:** The button is disabled when there are no defaulters. This means all students are up to date with payments. ğŸ‰

### Q: Can I email the PDF?

**A:** Yes! Save as PDF first, then attach to email and send.

### Q: How often should I generate this report?

**A:** Recommended: Weekly for monitoring, Monthly for formal records, End-of-term for audits.

### Q: What does "No filters applied" mean in the PDF?

**A:** It means the report shows ALL students with outstanding balances, not limited to any specific criteria.

### Q: The outstanding balance looks wrong. What should I do?

**A:** Verify that:

1. Fee configurations are correct
2. All payments have been recorded
3. Student is in correct class with correct fees

### Q: Can I print multiple copies?

**A:** Yes! Save as PDF once, then print the PDF as many times as needed.

### Q: What's the difference between Partial and Overdue?

**A:**

-   **Partial:** Some payment made, balance remaining (may or may not be past deadline)
-   **Overdue:** Past the payment deadline with outstanding balance (urgent!)
